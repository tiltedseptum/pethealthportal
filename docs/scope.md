# Scope

## Product
Browser-only responsive pet health portal for one pet first.

## Goal
Help a pet parent maintain continuity of pet health by keeping all important information in one place.

## Core modules
- Pet Profile
- Health Timeline
- Observations
- Preventive Care
- Medical Records

## Non-goals
- No native mobile app
- No clinic software
- No AI diagnosis
- No marketplace
- No multi-pet support in V1
- No vet collaboration workflows

## Success criteria
- Log a health event in under 60 seconds
- Retrieve a record in under 30 seconds
- Review last 2–4 weeks of health history easily
- Do not miss preventive care actions
